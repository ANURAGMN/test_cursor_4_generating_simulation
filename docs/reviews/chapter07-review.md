# Chapter 7 – Review (Pros, Cons, Improvements)

**Topic:** Heat – sources, conduction, conductors/insulators, convection, land/sea breeze, radiation, combined transfer, water cycle, infiltration, conservation  
**Files:** 10 simulations.

## Pros

- Clear physics: conduction, convection, radiation, and applications (breeze, water cycle, infiltration, conservation).
- Interactivity: timers, sliders, method tabs; all handlers (startConvection, stopConvection, setSource, selectMethod) defined and used.
- Real-world links: land/sea breeze, water cycle, recharge, conservation methods.

## Cons

- Land/sea breeze day/night toggle could have larger touch targets.
- Time toggles, source buttons, method tabs need aria-labels.

## Improvements

1. Add aria-labels to time buttons (day/night), source buttons, and method tabs.
2. Optional: Increase tap area for time and method tabs on mobile.
