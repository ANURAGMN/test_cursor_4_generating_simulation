Tap an object to strike it and hear what sound it makes!

Sonorous materials produce a ringing sound that sustains.
Non-sonorous materials produce a dull thud sound.
ðŸŽ¯ What You'll Learn
Metals are sonorous - they ring when struck! That's why bells, cymbals, and ghungroos are made of metal.

Non-metals like wood, plastic, and rubber produce dull sounds - they absorb vibrations instead of transmitting them.
