Select a material and hammer it to see if it's malleable or brittle!

üéØ What You'll Learn
Metals like copper, aluminium, and iron are malleable - they can be beaten into thin sheets (like silver foil on sweets!).

Non-metals like coal, sulfur, and chalk are brittle - they break when hammered.
