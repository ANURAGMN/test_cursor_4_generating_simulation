💧 States of Water

Same substance, different forms!

📚 Key Concept
Water exists in three states: solid (ice), liquid (water), and gas (steam). Changing states is a physical change - the substance (H₂O) remains the same!

• 0°C = Melting/Freezing point
• 100°C = Boiling/Condensation point

State changes are physical changes and are reversible. Heat ice → water → steam, then cool steam → water → ice!
