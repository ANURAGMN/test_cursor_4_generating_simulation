🔬 Properties of Acids & Bases

Learn what makes them different!

😋
Sour Taste

Acids taste sour or tart. That's why lemons, oranges, and vinegar have that tangy taste!

Common Examples
🍋 Citric acid 🍊 Ascorbic acid 🫙 Acetic acid 🥛 Lactic acid

Corrosive Nature

Strong acids can corrode metals and cause burns. They react with metals to produce hydrogen gas.

Examples
🔋 Battery acid 🧹 Cleaning acids
😝
Bitter Taste

Bases often taste bitter. But remember: not everything bitter is a base (like bitter gourd)!

Common Examples
🥄 Baking soda 💊 Antacids 🧼 ಸಾಬೂನು (don't taste!)

ಸಾಬೂನುy/Slippery Feel

Bases feel slippery or soapy. This is because they react with oils on your skin.

Examples
🧼 ಸಾಬೂನು solution 🧹 Detergent 🥄 Baking soda
