How to Prepare Rose Extract:
  1. Collect fresh red rose petals
  2. Crush petals with a little hot water
  3. Filter to get the colored extract
  4. Use this extract as a natural indicator!

üí° How Rose Extract Works

Red rose petal extract contains natural pigments that change color based on pH. It's a wonderful natural indicator that you can make at home!
