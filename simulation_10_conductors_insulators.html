üî¨ Why Some Materials Conduct?
Conductors have free electrons that can move easily, carrying electric current. Metals are excellent conductors because their atoms release electrons that flow freely.

Insulators hold their electrons tightly - they cannot move to carry current. That's why we use plastic to cover wires for safety!
